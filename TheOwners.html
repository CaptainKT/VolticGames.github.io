Input About Our Owners

The owners of VGC consist of two brothers and one best friend who had the idea one sunny afternoon of creating their very own gaming community that they could meet up with friends and be able to escape the real world problems of today. For more information about them take a look below! 

The Leaders

Jared "The FallenAngell" Bailey

Co-Owner / May - 2016

Here at Voltic Jared "The FallenAngell" Bailey was the first to dream the idea of having his very own gaming community since he is a gamer at heart. Jared makes a lot of the final decision calls after things have been talked about amongst the three leaders.

Kellen "Captain" Burke

Co-Owner / May - 2016

Here at Voltic Kellen "Captain" Burke has been apart of many gaming communities but none of them being creations of his own. Therefore when the idea was brought up he jumped right in to help out where he could. Kellen is incharge of the social media and graphics here at Voltic.

Trevor "The Law" Hall

Co-Owner / May - 2016

Here at Voltic Trevor "The Law" Hall is Jareds best friend and although he has not been apart of many gaming communities he knows a good amount about them and does a great amount of research here at Voltic to help make sure the community is on the right track.
